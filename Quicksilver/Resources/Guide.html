Welcome to Quicksilver!

Welcome to Quicksilver, a lightweight, fast and free application for improving productivity whilst on your Mac. Quicksilver gives you the control and power to save your time, act without doing, and work without effort.

Getting started

  • Read our Getting Started guide to get up to speed on how to use Quicksilver.
  • Set up your Catalog to choose the files and resources searchable from within Quicksilver.
  • Install the Recommended Plugins to extend Quicksilver's capabilities.
  • Set up Triggers to automate common tasks.
